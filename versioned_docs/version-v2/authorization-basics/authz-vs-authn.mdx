# Authorization vs Authentication

Authentication and Authorization are two terms that are often thought of as the same thing. In this article we'll attempt to clarify what what each of these terms mean and how they differ. In order to have a secure system, you need both. At a 25000 foot level, **Authentication** is concerned with _WHO you are_ and **Authorization** is concerned with _WHAT you can do_.
