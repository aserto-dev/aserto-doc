# Create an Aserto account and tenant

Go to [aserto.com/#signup](https://www.aserto.com/#signup) and complete the Sign Up experience.

<img src="/signup.png" alt="signup" width="500px" />

After signing up and verifying your email address, log in to your account.

![login](/login.png)

## Create your tenant

Once you have an account, you will be asked to create a tenant:

![create-tenant](/create-tenant.png)
